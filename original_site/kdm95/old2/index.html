    ABOUT

    Kyle Murray is a PhD Candidate at Cornell University in the department of Earth and Atmospheric Sciences. His research has focused broadly on several themes which are all linked by the drive to push the limits of geodetic methods to more precisely measure and map how the earth is actively deforming in space and time. See his research page for more details about past and current projects.
